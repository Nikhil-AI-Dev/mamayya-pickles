FAQS

Before you order

How long does delivery take?

We need at least 7 days from order to delivery. This includes 2–3 days for fresh preparation, 1 day for packing and resting, and 3–4 days for shipping via our courier partners. Once dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery?

No. Mamayya Pickles is online payment only. We accept UPI, credit cards, debit cards, and net banking via Razorpay. This helps us maintain quality and avoid returns on perishable meat products.

What's the shelf life?

Unopened jars last 3 months at room temperature. Once opened, refrigerate and consume within 3–4 weeks. Always use a dry spoon.

Do you ship across India?

Yes — we deliver to all serviceable pin codes across India via Shiprocket. Remote areas may take an extra day or two. Enter your pin code on any product page to check serviceability and the exact delivery estimate.

Is it safe to order meat pickle online?

Yes. We are FSSAI registered, source fresh meat, cook in controlled batches, vacuum-seal glass jars, and pack in tamper-evident boxes. The pickling process itself preserves the meat naturally with vinegar, salt, and oil.

What's your return policy?

Since this is a perishable food product, we do not accept returns. However, if your order arrives damaged or with a broken seal, please share an unboxing video within 24 hours and we'll send a replacement or refund. No questions asked.

Do you do bulk or gifting orders?

Yes! For weddings, corporate gifting, or 5kg+ orders, please email us at hello@mamayyapickles.in with your requirements. We offer custom packaging for bulk orders.
